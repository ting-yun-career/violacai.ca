Who am I?

Your Personal Wealth Advisor and Mortgage Broker

Viola is an insurance agent who believes in a holistic approach to financial well-being. Starting her journey in the financial services industry as an advisor with WFG in 2017, she developed a strong understanding of wealth management and financial planning. This foundation was further enhanced in 2023 when she acquired her mortgage agent license, providing her with a unique perspective on her clients' overall financial needs. Today, Viola helps clients across Canada, with a significant focus in the Greater Vancouver area, as well as growing client bases in Alberta and Ontario. She is dedicated to navigating the complexities of insurance to find the right coverage for their unique circumstances, no matter where they are in Canada.
